Why You Need Travel Insurance for Your Next Trip

Traveling can be exciting, but unexpected events such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost baggage can turn your dream trip into a nightmare. That’s where travel insurance comes in.

What Does Travel Insurance Cover?

  • Trip Cancellations – Get reimbursement if you have to cancel your trip due to unforeseen circumstances.
  • Medical Emergencies – Coverage for medical expenses and emergency evacuation.
  • Lost or Delayed Baggage – Compensation for lost, stolen, or delayed luggage.
  • Flight Delays – Get compensated for unexpected travel interruptions.
